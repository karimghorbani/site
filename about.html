karim Ghorbani,PhD

Dr. karim Ghorbani is a senior expert, working on integrated water resources management and conducting educational courses for staff and students, working at department of water and soil management and engineering affaires, ministry of agriculture-Jahad, Iran.He received his bachelor degree in water and soil engineering from Tabriz University at 1991, Iran, and continued his researches and studies and received Master of Science degree at 2002 from Chamran University, Iran. After 20 years working experiences in various fields and working positions and conducting several technical projects like dam to supply required water for agricultural purpose, sediment and flood control projects in watersheds, and designing over 70 schemes for implementation, decided to continue his study to complete PhD. He received PhD degree in water engineering from university putra Malaysia (upm) at 2016. His research area is water resources management, modeling of drainage systems, simulation of flooding prone area, river Engineering and etc. Beside of designing and studying several basic projects on drainage system, conservation, management of water resources, He has published several papers in international academic journals and conferences. This senior expert is writing two new books on drainage and hydrology that soon will be published.
